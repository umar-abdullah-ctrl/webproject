CYBER SECURITY

Cybersecurity is like a digital guard for computers, networks, and information. Its main job is to protect them from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage. It involves using various measures, like firewalls and encryption, to ensure that your digital world stays safe and secure. Just like you lock your doors to keep your home safe, cybersecurity helps keep your digital space protected from online threats.

Security Measures

Trojan Horses

Trojan Horses

To counter Trojan Horse attacks, use up-to-date antivirus software. This helps detect and remove malicious software, enhancing overall system security. Additionally, practice safe internet and email habits to avoid potential threats. Regular system scans and monitoring are essential for proactive defense.


Session Hijacking

Session Hijacking

To counter Session Hijacking, use HTTPS for encrypted data exchange. Secure coding practices, software updates, and implementing unique session identifiers with timeouts enhance protection against this type of attack.

DNS Spoofing

DNS Spoofing

To thwart DNS Spoofing, use DNS Security Extensions (DNSSEC) for cryptographic protection, ensuring data integrity. Regularly monitor DNS traffic for anomalies and rely on reputable DNS servers to minimize the risk of spoofing attacks.

URL Interpretation

URL Interpretation

To counter URL Interpretation attacks, enforce strict input validation in web applications. Regular security audits and penetration testing identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

MITM Attacks

MITM Attacks

To counter Man-in-the-Middle (MiTM) attacks, implement HTTPS for encrypted communication, use VPNs for secure channels, and regularly update systems to address vulnerabilities and enhance defense.
